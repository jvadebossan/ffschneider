Electrical safety and EMC compliance

Failing electrical safety or EMC tests can be both costly and time-consuming. With firsthand experience in these challenges, I can guide you through these complex standards and help fine-tune your hardware, firmware, and software to minimize failure risks while maintaining cost efficiency.

When needed, we can also conduct pre-compliance EMC or safety tests in certified laboratories, offering valuable insights at a relatively low cost to ensure your project stays on track.

Device and process documentation

With extensive experience in IEC 60601-1, IEC 60601-1-2, IEC TR 60601-4-2, IEC 62304, IEC 62471, IEC 60601-2-57, IEC 60601-2-18, FDA Software Requirements, and other key standards and guidances such as ISO 13495, I can help navigate complex regulatory landscapes.

I understand that no development holds value unless the device reaches the market. I can identify applicable regulatory requirements, present them clearly and concisely, and collaborate with you to develop the necessary documentation for your project's regulatory approval.

Cost-effective ISO and IEC labs

Brazil offers excellent ILAC-approved laboratories, whose reports are recognized by both MDR and FDA. These labs provide a cost-effective solution for testing your medical devices, often at a fraction of the cost typically found in the USA.

I have personally experienced this advantage, obtaining laboratory results at just 15% of the cost of a U.S. lab while successfully securing FDA approval for our medical device.
